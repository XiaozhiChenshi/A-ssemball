Antiprism Resources - Extras
============================

The extra resources are unsupported, and provided for interest only. They
may be useful as examples. Programs or scripts may require modification
before they will work.


python
------

o  anim_spin.py
     Makes a spinning polyhedron animation

o  helix.py
     Makes a helix by repeatedly bonding a polyhedron to itself

o  trunc_scale.py
     Makes an animation of the full range of an idealised truncation 

o  trunc_switch.py
     Makes an animation that switches between a dual pair by truncation


shell_scripts
-------------

o  demo.sh
     Bash script to display the demonstration models (Jean-Pierre Demailly)
     
o  dual_pair.sh
     Bash script to make a dual pair (Roger Kaufman)
